My biography


Hello I'm Jeremy Merlin, I am 28 years old, I come from France and actually living in Liverpool.

I just start a 10 weeks bootcamp at Tech Educators to get back into web development and start a career in tech.

I pass a Master of Informatic Engineer that I finish in 2018, but since I train and become a cocktail bartender for the last 4 years at Be at One.

During my spare time I go to gym, play video games and watch a lot of series and movies !
I used to pilot glider while I was in France but I stop since I'm living in UK.
